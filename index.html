Art direction, Brand Design, and Creative Coaching

Profile

Sara has spent over a decade working with leading global brands within multi-award-winning in-house creative teams, to bring advertising concepts and design ideas to life.

She brings broad experience across a variety of industries including fashion, health, and beauty. She is a 360 degree designer who provides nurturing leadership, conceptual thinking, creative art direction, intelligent copywriting, and integrated front-end development skills to the teams she has managed and coached.

Having reached a senior level at one of the longest-established, and largest (90 strong) full-service, internal creative agencies, she has successfully managed both the design and production teams at Specsavers Creative Agency. Her role as Head of Design saw her playing a key role in the design and implementation of the company’s re-brand, during which she guided design teams across multiple countries including Australia, the Nordics, and the UK

Sara currently provides creative mentorship to a number of mentees both locally and internationally, in addition to working as Lead Design Lecturer on Digital Jersey's Digital Leadership Programme.

UI, UX and Digital

Sara worked as the lead designer within Australia & New Zealand for Australia’s number one prestige skincare brand, Jurlique; as well as working in web design and front-end web development at Europe’s largest online beauty retailer, feelunique.com

She is proficient in CSS & HTML5 and was responsible for designing responsive weekly marketing newsletters (EDMs); creating content pages using CSS & flash applications such as 10CMS; mentoring designers in 10CMS & CSS, and ensuring that the team stay on brand. She created a range of graphics and monthly themes for use across the website & social media platforms, and gained skills in User Experience and Interface design.

Skills

Digital Textiles

Having polished her skills in graphic design over the past decade, Sara is fully competent using Adobe Cretive Suite including XD, Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesig, After Effects & Dreamweaver for both online design & print.

Traditional Textiles

In 2009 Sara was awarded Jersey Textile Showcase's "Award for Design" following on from her MText Degree, with a strong focus on hand drawing, art working & design development for both self designated and externally set briefs for clients such as Dorma and Turner Brothers. Her major project innovation was micro-encapsulated sustainable travel wear. She designs for target markets by matching key trends & colour palettes to market level and client needs, whilst maintaining creativity; and loves to generate new ideas and concepts in response to briefs and trend predictions.

Photography

In 2008 Sara won Societe Jersiaise Photographic Competition's "Architecture Award" and gained a commendation in the Natural History section, following on from an AS Level Photography evening course. This enabled her to begin work as a freelance photographer for Snapper and Gallery Magazine. In addition to private commissions, photography will always be one of her creative passions, and she enjoys combining it with other processes and incorporating it into her textile & graphic design projects.
